Chicken Noodle Soup

Embrace Cozy Comfort with a Warm Bowl of Joy!

Whether you're feeling under the weather or you're just in need of a little comfort, there's nothing like homemade chicken noodle soup to warm you up from the inside out. With this shortcut recipe, you can have the best chicken noodle soup of your life on the table in just 40 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can vegetable broth
  • 1 ½ cups egg noodles
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, & pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, & pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  5. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, & pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  7. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  8. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, & pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  9. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  10. Your Soup is READY!
